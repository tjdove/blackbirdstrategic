Who We Are

Built for
What's Next

Blackbird Strategic Holdings was founded by Matthew and Taylor Gill in May 2025 — not simply to take aerial photographs, but to pioneer what unmanned aircraft systems can do for commercial industry.

"Our vision is not to take pretty pictures of landscapes, but to pioneer new use-cases for UAS — and to integrate AI-driven monitoring systems in ways that deliver unprecedented value across industries."

We've made a personal investment in this space because we genuinely believe in where it's headed. Every job we fly today is building toward a larger mission: making UAS an indispensable tool for the businesses that shape the Southeast.
